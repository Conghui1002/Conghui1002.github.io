I'm a postdoctoral research fellow at ICSI/UC Berkeley with Prof. Stella Yu. My research focuses on sketch understanding including sketch recognition and sketch-based image retrieval. I am also interested in human visual perception.

I received my Ph.D. degree (2014-2018) from QMUL/SketchX Lab, advised by Dr. Yi-Zhe Song and Prof. Tao Xiang. I graduated from BUPT with a B.S. degree in Telecommunications Engineering with Management.
