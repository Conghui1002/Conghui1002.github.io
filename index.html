I am currently a PhD student within the SketchX Research Lab and the Computer Vision Group in the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary, University of London, jointly supervised by Dr. Yi-zhe Song, Dr. Tao Xiang, and Dr. Timothy Hospedales .

Before joining the Computer Vision Group in QMUL I received my B.Sc. in Electronics and Information Engineering from Dalian University of Technology. Then after 3 years research in image and speech processing, I received my M.Sc in AAU Lab at Dalian University of Technology, jointly supervised by Prof. Xiaohong Ma and Prof. Shuxue Ding.

Currently I am interested in sketch-based image retrieval using Deep Learning. I am now developing framework learning cross-domain representation for sketch and photo modality, and then make fine-grained retrieval based on the learned representation. Besides, fine-grained attributes are also exploited in the multi-task learning.
